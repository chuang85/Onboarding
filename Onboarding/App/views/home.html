AAD Onboarding Wiki

So you want to onboard your application to Azure Active Directory? Great, let's get started!

Do you already have an application provisioned? If so, you can update the app's properties or add a certificate.

If not, you will need to provide us with some information so we can get you up and running. Before that happens it is important to understand that this is not a one-size-fits-all process. There are many factors that will determine the best way to deploy your application and secure your resources; having a crystal clear picture of the scenario(s) relevant to your app will expedite the process and ensure the best solution is provided for you and your customers.
